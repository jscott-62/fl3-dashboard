# FL3 Competitor Intel - Competitive Intelligence System

Monitors and analyzes competing programs in the crypto education, retirement catch-up, and Bitcoin education spaces. Updates the competitive landscape and identifies FL3 positioning opportunities.

## When to Use This Skill

- Monthly competitive review
- When a new competitor enters the space
- Before a launch (know what the market is doing)
- When the Strategy Advisor agent needs competitive context

## Complete Workflow

### Phase 1: Load Existing Analysis

Read `Main/FL3 ZenithPro Data/Competitor Analysis - Crypto Education Programs.md`

### Phase 2: Accept Intel

1. **New competitor URLs to research** (if any)
2. **Market developments** (new programs, pricing changes, positioning shifts)
3. **Specific questions** (e.g., "What are competitors charging?" or "Who's targeting 50+?")

### Phase 3: Analyze Against FL3 Positioning

For each competitor, evaluate:

| Dimension | FL3 | Competitor |
|---|---|---|
| Target age | 45+ | [Their age] |
| Positioning | Education, not advice | [Their positioning] |
| Price point | $497-$697 | [Their price] |
| Core asset | Bitcoin + tech stocks | [Their focus] |
| Differentiator | Dignity-first, catch-up specific | [Their differentiator] |
| Tone | Calm authority, no hype | [Their tone] |

### Phase 4: Identify Opportunities

1. Gaps in the market FL3 can fill
2. Messaging advantages FL3 has
3. Pricing positioning relative to competitors
4. Content topics competitors are missing
5. Audience segments competitors are ignoring

### Phase 5: Produce Report

```markdown
## FL3 COMPETITIVE INTELLIGENCE: [Date]

### COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE
| Competitor | Target | Price | Positioning | FL3 Advantage |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| [Name] | [Who] | [$X] | [What] | [Why FL3 wins] |

### NEW DEVELOPMENTS
- [What changed since last review]

### OPPORTUNITIES
1. [Positioning opportunity]
2. [Content gap to exploit]
3. [Audience segment to capture]

### THREATS
1. [Competitive threat to monitor]

### RECOMMENDATIONS
1. [Specific action to take]
```

Save updated analysis to `reports/`
